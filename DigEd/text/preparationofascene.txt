Preparation of a Scene
5 W's
1) Who are you? Who are you talking to? What is your relationship to them?
2) What do you want? What is your objective? What are you trying to get or
accomplish?
3) When does the scene take place? Time of day/night. Year, (approximately)
4) Where are you? Describe location in detail. Use the five senses: Sight, Smell
Sound, Touch, Taste.
5) Why are you here? What is your purpose in the scene for being there?
6) What are some of the feelings/emotions your character is going through during the
scene? Name at least three.
7) What is your character's biggest fear in the scene?
8) Mark your scripts so that you can find your lines easily--highlight, circle, etc.
